FOOD

Food is any substance consumed to provide nutritional support and energy for the body. It contains nutrients such as carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals, and water that help us grow, stay healthy, and perform daily activities.

  • Main Types of Food

  • Carbohydrates
    • – Provide energy (e.g., Rice, bread, potatoes).
  • Proteins
    • – Help build and repair tissues (e.g., Egg, fish, beans).
  • Fats
    • – Store energy and support cell function (e.g., nuts, oils, avocados).
  • Vitamins and Minerals
    • – Support various body functions (e.g., fruits and vegetables).
    • Water – Essential for hydration and bodily processes.

  • Benefits of Healthy Food9

  1. Provides energy.
  2. Supports growth and development.
  3. Strengthens the immune system.
  4. Helps maintain a healthy weight.
  5. Reduces the risk of many diseases.

Examples of Healthy Foods

  1. Apple
  2. Broccoli
  3. Brown Rice
  4. Salmon
